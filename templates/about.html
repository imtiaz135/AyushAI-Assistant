About AYUSH AI Assistant

AYUSH represents the traditional systems of medicine in India, including Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy. These systems focus on holistic health and natural healing and play a vital role in India’s healthcare ecosystem.

Ayush AI Assistant is designed to ensure the authenticity and quality of educational content related to AYUSH. By leveraging Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and intelligent analysis, the system detects inconsistencies, errors, and misleading information in textbooks and documents.

Why This Platform?

  • Prevent misinformation in AYUSH education
  • Ensure accuracy of medical knowledge
  • Support students and educators
  • Improve quality of reference materials
